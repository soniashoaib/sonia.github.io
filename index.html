01 / 07

Looking Out for You

Oil Paint on Wood

This piece explores the feelings of nostalgia associated with childhood and siblings, which is painted with warm and bright colours. This is juxtaposed with the feelings of emptiness that follow when siblings leave and you grow up, which is painted in cool colours.

02 / 07

Rippling Reflection

Oil Paint on Acrylic Pour Art on Canvas

This piece aims to explore themes of fluidity and identity, as the background is loose and flowy while the foreground is precise and controlled.

03 / 07

Fair Game

Acrylic Paint on Wood

This piece aims to convey South Asian beauty standards around the idea of being pale and having light skin. The background is red to convey a sense of control, while the three women in the foreground apply white foundation and makeup to their faces.

04 / 07

Control Yourself

Acrylic Paint on Canvas with Yarn

This piece is a study of identity and autonomy, using various different techniques and colouring to portray a sense of conflict and themes of control.

05 / 07

Mera Ghar

Acrylic Paint & Mixed Media on Wooden Skateboard

This piece is representative of my cultural identity, being Pakistani and growing up in Singapore. It aims to show the feeling of cognitive dissonance between cultures and identity. The phrase “Mera Ghar” is painted and means “My Home”.

06 / 07

Unveil

Pen, Charcoal & Watercolour on Paper

This piece aims to represent me as a person, with a hand removing a veil to represent me revealing my identity. The skull is representative of the theme of mortality, while the chess pieces on top of the book aim to show surface-level aspects of my identity while the rest is concealed.

07 / 07

Study after Nicole Sleeth

Oil Paint on Canvas

A study of Nicole Sleeth's portraiture, undertaken to refine oil painting and portraiture skills.

✦ About the Artist

Sonia Shoaib

I am a 17-year-old artist who has been dedicated to creating paintings since I was 12. I have experimented with various sketching and painting techniques, allowing me to explore balancing technical accuracy with artistic expression.

I have also studied various artists to draw inspiration from their techniques to learn them and therefore blend my ideas together into an original work of art.

Oil & acrylic paint, watercolour, charcoal, pen & mixed media
5 years of dedicated practice
Based in Singapore
